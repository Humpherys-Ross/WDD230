Design Principles

Ross Humpherys

Fitts's Law

TCG Player

TCG Player

This website demonstrates Fitt's law. The places to click are large and they clearly denote what, and where, they go to. The other targets on the webpage are also large and easy to click.

Whitespace and Clean Design

Prezi

https://prezi.com

This website demonstrates the use of whitespace and clean design. The webiste is very minimal, only giving you the exact information you need with very little extra embellishment. The website is also very easy to navigate, with a simple menu bar at the top of the page. The website is also very easy to read, with a simple colour scheme and large font sizes.

Proximity

Runescape Wiki

https://runescape.wiki

This website demonstrates Proximity. Everything is grouped together, with a navigation bar to the left. In the middle, everything is grouped together. For the skill training, all the guides are grouped together, making it easy to navigate the page.
